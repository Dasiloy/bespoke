ABOUT ME

When I finally started taking my strengths seriously and figured out how to put them to work, I landed my dream career as a learning & development facilitator!

Two years later, I had the opportunity to begin training leadership development courses, quickly falling in love with the chance to help others become better leaders. Enjoying countless opportunities to discuss strategic work and provide a much more significant influence to lead others with confidence, I knew that I'd found my true passion in life. My genuine love for leadership development would only continue growing over the years as I worked hard on improving my coaching skills to become a high-energy leader who cares deeply for both people and dogs. Known for challenging convention while honoring diversity and inspiring the change needed to get people to dream big, I believe in bringing big things to the business coaching and career coaching industries. In addition, I've earned a solid reputation for expressing my strong values on feminism, anti-capitalism, anti-racism, dismantling social injustices, and more.

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Bespoke Development is a coaching company with a laser focus on career path design and leadership development.

We're driven by a strong belief in the importance of encouraging and supporting others to become the best versions of themselves.

Business professionals looking to grow their careers or improve their leadership skills through coaching and training can benefit from the services we provide to our clients.
